## 2024-07-14 - Custom Modals Require Manual Accessibility Wiring
**Learning:** Custom modals built from `div`s (like `#screenshotModal`, `#pickerModal`, `#systemMapModal`) do not inherit native dialog behaviors. They require manual wiring of semantic ARIA attributes (`role="dialog"`, `aria-modal="true"`), proper `aria-label`s on both the container and close buttons, and keyboard event listeners (like global `Escape` key dismissal and `Enter`/`Space` activation on non-button close icons) to meet accessibility standards and provide intuitive UX.
**Action:** When building or enhancing custom modals in this design system, always ensure they implement `role="dialog"`, `aria-modal="true"`, semantic close buttons with `aria-label`s, and an `Escape` key event listener that calls the component's native close function.
